ABOUT ME

HELLO!

I am an Electronics and Communications engineering undergrad, and an IoT and robotics enthusiast, exploring the fields of Embedded microcontroller programming, Device-to-Cloud communications, data visualization and analytics.

STUDENT LIFE

Learning through applying concepts practically is what I aim for, and have worked on various realtime scenario projects which has made me proficient in programming languages like C, C++, Python, Java and microcontrollers and minicomputers such as the Arduino UNO, Raspberry Pi, ESP8266, ESP32, ATMEL AT89S52, PIC16f877a. I have also attended various seminars, webinars and have been part of the PR and Marketing team for NIIT University's tech fest "SiNuSoid v2.0".

"Sharing is caring"- the Community Connect programme at NIIT University has provided every student the opportunity to educate and help the poor by conducting trips to nearby government schools.

PROFESSIONAL LIFE

Success is at the forefront of every professional decision you make. The beginning of my professional journey has been at Intugine Technologies, where exploration and experimentation are the driving forces. Having the opportunity to work in the Research & Development (R&D) division of the IoT team, I have had the opportunity to test and develop various prototypes using microcontrollers such as the STM32F1XX family, STM32F41XX family, STM32F40XX family, Raspberry Pi Pico, Ai Thinker - A9/A9G . I have also been assigned with the task of product implementation at the client site where my role has been to demonstrate the working and handling of devices to ensure ease of utilization.

INTERESTS

Apart from academic oriented activites, I like to spend some time on my hobbies - listening to music, sketching, playing the guitar, gaming (Multiplayer/Singleplayer), reading books, playing outdoor games.
