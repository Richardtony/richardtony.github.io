ABOUT ME

HELLO!

I am an Electronics and Communications engineering undergrad, and an IoT and robotics enthusiast, exploring the fields of Embedded microcontroller programming, Device-to-Cloud communications, data visualization and analytics.

STUDENT LIFE

Learning through applying concepts practically is what I aim for, and have worked on various realtime scenario projects which has made me proficient in programming languages like C, C++, Python, Java and hardware programmable boards such as the Arduino UNO, Raspberry Pi, ESP8266, ESP32, ATMEL AT89S52, PIC16f877a. I have also attended various seminars, webinars and have been part of the PR and Marketing team for NIIT University's tech fest "SiNuSoid v2.0".

"Sharing is caring"- the Community Connect programme at NIIT University has provided every student the opportunity to educate and help the poor by conducting trips to nearby government schools.

INTERESTS

Apart from academic oriented activites, I like to spend some time on my hobbies - listening to music, playing the guitar, gaming (Multiplayer/Singleplayer), reading books, playing outdoor games.
